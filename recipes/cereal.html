Milk and Cereal

A Real Crowd Pleaser

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Cereal (e.g., Fruity Pebbles, etc.)
  • Ponzu
  • Dashi Broth

Instructions

  1. Get bowl.
  2. Open Milk jug.
  3. Pour cereal in bowl.
  4. Pour milk over cereal (in the bowl).
  5. Get spoon.
  6. Put Ponzu and Dashi back in the fridge.
